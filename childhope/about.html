Our Vision

ChildHope Foundation envisions a world where children flourish in family care, safe from threats to their well-being.

Our Mission

ChildHope Foundation's mission is to place children in loving families for their safety and well-being.

Our Purpose

Is to ensure children are raised in loving families rather than institutions. At the heart of our mission is the belief that every child deserves love, security, shelter, health and education. We are driven by our commitment to protect children from the harmful effects of institutional care.
