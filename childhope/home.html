Our vision is a world where children thrive in family care and are free from threats to their survival.

Adoption

We offer support to couples in Malaysia throughout the adoption process, helping them every step of the way. At ChildHope Foundation, we assist you in finding the ideal match for your future children and provide three different adoption options. Allow us to assist you as you begin your adoption process.

Reintegration

Reintegration is the process of carefully returning a kid from an institution to their family. A kid may be adopted if they are unable to reintegrate with their biological parents or other family members. Child safety and well-being are the top priorities of ChildHope's reintegration programme.

Counselling

The ChildHope Foundation offers therapy to specific clients, including young girls and women dealing with an unwanted pregnancy as well as children and their families going through the reintegration process. The client receives counselling to assist them deal with a possibly extremely stressful circumstance.
